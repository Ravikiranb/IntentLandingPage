How it works

Simple to start.
Powerful over time.

Intent takes seconds to set up and gets smarter the more you use it.

1

Grant Screen Time access

Intent uses Apple's FamilyControls and DeviceActivity APIs to read your usage. Setup takes under a minute and everything stays on your device.

2

Set your daily goal

Tell Intent how much total screen time you want each day. It learns which apps are distracting you most and surfaces them first.

3

Run a Focus Session

Pick a duration, choose which apps to block, and go. A Live Activity shows your timer on the Dynamic Island while you work.

4

Review your week

Every week Intent generates a report of your progress — your Focus Score trend, top distractions, and AI-written insights on what to change.

Privacy

Your data never
leaves your phone.

Privacy isn't a policy checkbox for us — it's a design constraint.

🔒

Fully on-device

All screen time data, journal entries, and focus history stay on-device (SwiftData / Room). No servers, no accounts required.

📱

Native APIs only

Intent uses native frameworks (DeviceActivity on iOS, UsageStatsManager on Android) to query usage securely. No custom profiles or root access required.

🚫

No ads. Ever.

Intent is a paid product. Your attention is what you're trying to protect — we'd never sell it back to advertisers.

🔍

Open to inspect

The architecture and data models are fully documented. No hidden analytics SDKs, no third-party tracking.
